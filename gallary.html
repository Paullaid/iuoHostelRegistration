iuo gallery

The Gallery Section of Igbinedion University, Okada, is a dynamic space celebrating creativity and cultural expression. It showcases student and faculty artwork, photography, sculptures, and multimedia installations through rotating exhibitions. The gallery serves as a platform for students to present their work, fostering a vibrant artistic community.
The Gallery also hosts guest artists, workshops, and themed displays, fostering artistic dialogue and inspiration. Open to the university community and visitors, it reflects IUO’s commitment to arts, innovation, and intellectual engagement.
