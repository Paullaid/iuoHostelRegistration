Frequently Asked Questions

What types of hostels are available?

The university offers separate hostels for male and female students, equipped with modern amenities to ensure comfort and safety.

What are the benefits of staying in the university hostel?

Staying in the hostel provides convenience with close access to campus facilities, a secure environment, opportunities to build friendships, and a focused atmosphere for academic success.

How is security managed in the hostels?

The university provides 24/7 security personnel and surveillance systems to ensure the safety of all hostel residents.

Can I request special accommodations in the hostel?

Yes, students with special needs or requests can contact the hostel management office to discuss accommodations.

How do I apply for hostel accommodation?

Applications for hostel accommodation are submitted during the admission process or at the start of each academic session through the university's accommodation office.
