About Our University

History of Igbinedion University, Okada

Igbinedion University, Okada (IUO) holds the distinction of being Nigeria‚Äôs first private university, established on September 10, 1999, by Sir (Chief) Gabriel Osawaru Igbinedion, the Esama of Benin. The university was licensed by the Federal Government of Nigeria under the Education (National Minimum Standards and Establishment of Institutions) Act and began academic activities in 2000.

Founding Vision

Sir (Chief) Gabriel Osawaru Igbinedion, a prominent businessman and philanthropist, envisioned a world-class institution that would provide quality, accessible, and morally sound education to Nigerian and international students. The university was established to complement government efforts in higher education and reduce the pressure on public universities.

Early Development

  • First Location: The university started with temporary facilities in Okada, Edo State, before moving to its permanent site along the Benin-Lagos Expressway.
  • Pioneering Faculties: It commenced operations with three faculties: Arts & Social Sciences, Law, and Natural & Applied Sciences.
  • First Graduation: The maiden convocation held in 2005, marking a milestone in private university education in Nigeria.

Growth and Expansion

Igbinedion University, Okada (IUO) has over the years, expanded its academic offerings, infrastructure, and student population. Key developments include:

  • New Faculties & Programs: Added faculties like Health Sciences, Engineering, and Management Sciences, offering undergraduate and postgraduate degrees.
  • Affiliation & Accreditation: All programs are accredited by the National Universities Commission (NUC) and relevant professional bodies.
  • Notable Facilities: Features modern lecture halls, laboratories, a teaching hospital (Igbinedion University Teaching Hospital), and a vibrant gallery section for arts and culture.

Contributions to Education

As Nigeria‚Äôs first private university, IUO paved the way for other private institutions. It emphasizes:

  • Entrepreneurship & Innovation
  • Research & Community Development
  • Global Partnerships with universities abroad

Present-Day IUO

Today, Igbinedion University, remains a leading private institution known for its academic excellence, discipline, and contributions to national development. It continues to uphold its founder‚Äôs vision while adapting to modern educational demands.
